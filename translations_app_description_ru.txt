The unofficial FAN Project App A-Guide for the game ARK: Survival Evolved is a taming calculator and companion app for the ingame dino tame prozess. 
He calculated the required time and resources necessary for the dino taming. You can look at the effectiveness, the associated expected bonus level and extended stats for each food type. 
Plan your next taming with exact values and more fun with A-Guide. Survive now in Ark: Survival Evolved World. Good Luck and have Fun for dinosaur taming.
